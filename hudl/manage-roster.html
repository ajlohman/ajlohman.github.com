Disable Athlete

Click Disable if you want to remove a player from your roster but preserve their highlights and statistics in Hudl.

Use Delete if you want to permanently remove any tie from that player to your team.

Use the Disable button, if you want the athlete to retain their team and player highlights but have no access to the team library.
